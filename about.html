About Decoded Solutions

Founded by Ndimphiwe Given Bontsi, a Senior Java Engineer with 13 years of experience, dedicated to bridging the digital divide.

Our Story

Growing up in Nxuba, I watched local businesses struggle while big city companies took their customers. I knew technology could help but it felt out of reach for most people.

With over 13 years of experience as a Senior Java Engineer, I founded Decoded Solutions to make professional IT services accessible to small businesses and townships. We don't just provide technology—we empower communities.

Our mission is to create employment opportunities for youth while helping small businesses compete in the digital economy. Every project we take on includes training and knowledge transfer.

Our Mission

To democratize technology by making professional IT solutions accessible and affordable for small businesses and youth in underserved communities.

Our Vision

To become the leading IT partner for small businesses and townships across South Africa, empowering communities through technology while creating employment opportunities for youth.
